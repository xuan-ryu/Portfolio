Hi, I'm XUYUAN (徐源) — a UX designer and researcher with a focus on human-computer interaction, interactive media, and cultural storytelling.

My work spans digital interfaces, immersive experiences, and social impact design. Whether I'm redesigning a hiring platform powered by AI, building a VR experience about monarch butterflies, or reimagining how researchers access decades of public opinion data — I ask the same question: what does this feel like to the person using it?

I approach every project with the curiosity of a researcher and the care of a craftsperson. I believe design is never neutral — every interface embeds assumptions about who has agency, who the system is for, and what matters.
